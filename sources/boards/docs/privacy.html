Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy details how your private information is used by the following Google Chrome Browser extensions

  • Boards for Trello
  • New Tab Page for Trello

When you authorise the extension for use with Trello, I collect your email address and register it at http://paulferrett.com for one or more of the following reasons:

  1. To check if you should be upgraded to the Pro version without payment
  2. To subscribe you to my email newsletter, from which you can unsubscribe at any time.

I will never send spam, junk, or irrelevant email to you, and I will never give your email out to anyone else, unless required by law -- which I very much doubt will ever happen.

Third party services used by the extension:

  • Trello, Inc, is used for obvious reasons. See https://trello.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics is used to monitor extension usage statistics
  • Google Wallet is used to capture payments online for upgrading to Pro
  • See details regarding Google's privacy policy at https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/

This privacy policy is subject to change without notice and was last updated on 15 April 2015. If you have any questions feel free to contact me directly at paul@paulferrett.com.

This was not written by a lawyer, and is intended to honestly outline what I do with your data. I promise I will never intentionally do bad things with your data. Please don't sue me.
